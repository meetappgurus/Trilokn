How is IP handled in cross-border deployments?

Our global legal framework ensures 100% IP assignment to the client from the moment of creation. We manage Master Service Agreements (MSAs) that comply with international jurisdictions, ensuring all intellectual property is secured and fully transferred.

What defines an 'Expert' at Trilokn Infotech?

An 'Expert' at Trilokn Infotech is defined by our 50-point vetting matrix. This includes advanced algorithmic problem solving, proficiency in architecture design patterns, high emotional intelligence (EQ) for collaboration, and proven experience in high-growth engineering environments.

How does the 14-day trial period work?

The 14-day trial period is a zero-risk commitment. You can integrate a vetted expert into your team for two weeks. If you are not satisfied with their technical performance or team fit, you pay nothing and we provide an immediate replacement.

How is timezone overlap managed?

We guarantee a minimum of 4 hours of overlap with your core team's working hours, regardless of the expert's location. This ensures seamless synchronous communication while maintaining the benefits of a global distributed model.
